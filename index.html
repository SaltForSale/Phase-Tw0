CLOTHES A SECOND CHANCE
At Phase Tw0, we strive to address and spread the word about ongoing environmental issues caused by fast fashion and clothing waste. Every shopaholic has experienced the sight of their purchases piling up without a wear, and our mission is to prevent those garments from ending up in the landfill.

HOW IT WORKS


  • Donate your unwanted clothing to one of our locations (work in progress: please check our Instagram @phasetw0 for updates).


  • DONATE

  • Our team will hand-select the best donated garments for you! Each month, we will host a local thrift for cheap prices, and all funds will go towards reducing clothing waste.

  • THRIFT

  • Whatever doesn't get sold locally will be sent to thredUP to be either resold again or recycled.
